2. Booking & Registration

To reserve any destination or package, you must register a valid user account and log in. All bookings are subject to availability. You agree to provide accurate and complete personal details (name, email, and phone number) during booking.

3. Pricing & Payments

All prices stated on our platform are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are subject to change. Online payments are secured and processed through our verified gateway partner (Razorpay). For callback options, bookings remain pending until confirmed by our agent and payment is secured.

4. Travel Responsibilities & Insurance

Travelers are responsible for maintaining valid identification documents, visas, and health certifications during travel. Bharat Safar does not provide travel insurance. We highly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance prior to your trip.

5. Limitation of Liability

Bharat Safar acts as an intermediary connecting clients with service providers (hotels, transport, flights). We shall not be held liable for injuries, damage, delays, or unexpected adjustments caused by weather conditions, political situations, or other force majeure incidents.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and account passwords to create your account and handle travel reservations. Payment transaction details are securely processed by Razorpay and never stored on our servers.

2. Use of Information

Your collected information is strictly utilized to process transactions, confirm bookings, send automated invoices (PDF), notify you regarding tour status updates via notifications, and improve customer support responses.

3. Cookies Policy

Our website utilizes local storage and cookies to maintain active login sessions, store user theme preferences (Light / Dark mode), and retain security tokens. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but it will impact core reservation features.

4. Security & Data Protection

We employ standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, firewalls, and MongoDB Atlas secure cluster authentication to guard your private database from unauthorized access, modifications, or breaches.

1. Standard Refund Terms

Cancellations must be requested via the support hotline or email. Refunds are calculated based on the timing of the request prior to the scheduled travel date:

  • More than 15 Days prior: 90% Refund of the package price.
  • 7 to 15 Days prior: 50% Refund of the package price.
  • Less than 7 Days prior: No Refund. (100% cancellation fee applies).

2. Refund Process Timeline

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment source (bank account or card) within 5 to 7 business days. You will receive a system notification once the refund transaction is initiated from our side.

3. Tour Postponement

Travelers may request to reschedule their travel date up to 10 days before departure without any penalty, subject to availability in seasonal bookings and hotel pricing adjustments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book a tour package?

A: To book, you must first register and log in. Scroll to the "Popular Destinations" or "Packages" section, choose your destination, click "Book", select your dates, choose online payment or request a callback, and submit.

Q: What payment options do you support?

A: We support online payments (Cards, UPI, Netbanking, Wallets) via Razorpay secure gateway, or you can choose "Pay at Counter" / callback where our experts call to arrange offline payment.

Q: Can I customize my travel itinerary?

A: Yes! If your desired destination is not listed or you need custom arrangements, select "Other / General Query" from the destination dropdown, enter your details in the message box, and we will contact you within 24 hours.

Q: Where can I download my booking invoices?

A: Click on your Profile/Dashboard name in the top navigation, navigate to "My Bookings", and for any paid bookings, you will see a "Download PDF Invoice" button next to it.
